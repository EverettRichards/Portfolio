1/28/23

I look at the stars
The stars do the same
I promise them wonder
They promise me fame

A promise in Heaven
Falls deaf to my call
For nobody listens
To teardrops so small

They might be called miracles
They might be called saints
They might just as easy
Be called golden paints

A blind man can see
An alloy improper
For a plating of platinum
Is still only copper
