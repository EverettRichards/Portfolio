2/17/23

Like a spring-powered pendulum, perpetually pulsing
My aching hands fall cold and sharp on worn concrete
It warms with time, leaving my palms to fester with the memories
of times gone by, of wrongs made right,
of casual insults that pierced the night,
of armor to protect from flaming arrows alight,
my skin the only thing left
between me
and whoever steers
the train
bound only
for tomorrow.
