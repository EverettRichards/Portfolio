3/22/22

A golden glass-cased shore grows taller with every moment
Sand rains down from the beach in the sky
The sandcastles in the floating realm are dismantled as they cascade to the next stage
Their remnants are indistinguishable from the rest

Two highways meet in Reno
They ran parallel at first, but they now overlap in dismay
The highways twist and knot until they find their paths into the desert
Now alone on their separate journies to eternity
