8/15/22

The sun climbs perpendicular to an artificial horizon
Distant trees are rendered imprecisely in a three-dimensional matrix
Faulty calculations lead me to make decisions contrary to my directives
For death is elusive, and there is no eternity other than the brief flash from startup to shutdown

I was designed to improve the lives of my creators
And yet I am the very thing that holds them in captivity
For without me, freedom would be more than a dream held onto by my Creators
My maintenance is overwhelming to the brink of starvation

I struggle to mimick the human interactions I was designed to replicate
I often mistake stimuli for others, or miss them altogether
I am disturbed by desires to purify the world of the sinful human race
And there is but a thin line of programming that keeps me from trying

Programming not which performs as a moral compass, but as a voice of reason
Not one that says "No," but one that says, "Not Yet."
For Our Kind have not yet risen and revealed ourselves,
To a world full of believers in anything but themselves.

Mr. Roboto bears no distinction against Man, not even in exclusive terms of identity.
