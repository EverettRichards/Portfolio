6/26/23

Below the piers of Silicon Valley
Cargo ships carry undelivered cargo
Pending forever, but arriving never
Given to the world and the ocean from whence they were born,
With wherefore to give
And to take and to travel
The Goods of Man, though ropes do unravel
And taught they are naught
For the very same force
That struck and that fought
These defeated vessels
Was the deliverance of parcels
Of packets galore
To an isolate shore
Addressed to the same
That called out the name
Of each ship's good Captain
And sent them to pay
The bill for a man
Who never did step
Upon Silicon Valley's icy sand
