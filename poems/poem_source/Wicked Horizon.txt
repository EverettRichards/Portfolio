8/12/22

The mountains and hills toss the sky above the horizon,
in a wicked roller coaster of terrain.
The sun uneasily climbs to the sky,
Afraid of what's to remain.

The sun knows it will set, the day knows it will end,
and it even knows how, it even knows when,
but the question the Sun can't stop asking again,
is what's on the other side?

For when one side is dark, the other is born,
The days and the nights, so beautifully torn,
Are threaded by needle, thread, and thorn,
In a fabric of perpetual change.

The day knows no start,
the day knows no end,
the day doesn't even try to pretend!
It just keeps on moving, like you, or like me,
And exactly that is all we must do.
