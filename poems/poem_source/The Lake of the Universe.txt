3/18/22

A thousand silver orbs trickle down a frozen plane
Dark clouds overhead offer their tentative foresight
The sky offers a halfhearted apology to the drowning flowers of the valley
The flowers respond with hesitant gratitude, disguising their frowns with uneasy smiles

An overflowing lake reflects a figure dark and small
The figure gazes into the visions of the future offered by the smooth and icy surface
The ice cracks, splitting the silhouette on a jagged axis
The rain continues to fall without the help of the apprehensive figure

A mighty celestial column betrays the gloom
The valley is illuminated by the graceful warriors of the sky
The flowers rejoice in the generous sunlight that they are now afforded
The pulse of the Earth is restored as the valley awaits the first Autumn breeze

The ice in the lake melts at the sight of the day star's gift
The day-blind moon rises in approval of its mother's gesture
An eternal summer lies ahead for the two halves of the figure,
united at last in the Lake of the Universe.
