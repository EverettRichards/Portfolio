*Inspired by the song "And She Was" by The Talking Heads

I was looking for a person
With whom to share my world
And my everyday wonder
And you were.

Someone to care
When others did not
To be there for me
And you were.

A friend and a lover
To kiss and to hold
To love me in turn
And you were.

A beacon of hope
In a world of fear
And enveloping dark
And you were.

A final response
To questions once asked
But now surely answered
And you were.
