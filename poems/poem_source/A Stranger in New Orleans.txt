3/30/26

How strange is this place,
where distant strangers meet momentarily,
their web-like lives of infinite complexity
intersecting at a point, as conjoined manifolds?

Strangers come and acquaintances go.
Though we met eyes only for a brief moment,
we share a common understanding;
though we walked in parallel for one mere evening,
we are friends nonetheless;
connected by our respective similarities,
enriched by our respective differences.

People come from the world around for reasons abound,
and yet we all ended up here,
in the same city,
in the same hostel,
in the same billiards hall,
at the very same time.
