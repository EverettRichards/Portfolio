3/18/22

A faded golden line divides an endless black strand,
carelessly applied to an otherwise vacant landscape
A forgotten highway connects two distant towns,
but nobody is certain which town lies on the other side.

Some choose to believe a paradise lies at the end of the line
Others suggest that the road leads to a towering cliff overlooking a polluted sea
Some wonder if the forgotton highway eventually returns to its beginning
A few question whether or not the road even exists to begin with.

An intellectual drought sweeps over the forgotten town on the forgotten highway
The citydwellers relocate underground to affirm their imagined limits
The people look inside themselves for the answers they will never find,
for the city on the other side of the hill has done just the same.
