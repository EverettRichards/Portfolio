8/12/22

A brilliant satin shield protects the silent whispers of the night
An urban gradient taints the moon's enveloping cloak
The sky is blind to the stars, who hide behind an unwavering mask
Their light waiting behind a contrast-breaking curtain

The stars only dare to shine when there is nothing left to be seen
In a desert, on a plain, only miles and miles from the world
On no crowded street can one catch a glimpse of the heavens, shimmering overhead
For the city is as oblivious to the stars as a cartoon is to the viewer
