2/6/23

As I striketh my desk
As the ink streaks along
My hands are both broken
As they've been for long

My left hand is shattered
My right hand is frail
My mind is a tempest
My head like a veil

My thumbs weave like arrows
Their nocks all astrung
For all they can render
Are words often sung

Still young and still learning
Still looking for reason
We idly watch
As the sun changes season

And try as I might
To write senseful scrawl
The words on my paper
Mean nothing at all
