8/12/22

Ideas come often, they make hours whole
They give us a reason to focus control
They direct, they inspire, they break through the tolls,
They make us more human; together; whole.

But they evade my every attempt to grasp them
Except in the shallow waters before each night's rest
Surfacing only when they are certain to be forgotten.
They are who I am, and exist, they do not.
