8/24/22

Before me stretches not a path, but a continuous, many-angled wedge
My trajectory will be uncertain for many years hence
Alas I work and I tire, I indulge and perspire
With little to show recompense

Which way should I choose? Is it too late to turn?
Have the bridges of choice been set to a burn?
Will illusion and color help me to learn?
Or will I sit back 'til it is my turn?

The reaper waits not for those who relax,
For those who preach faith and those who preach facts,
The ones who're devoted to truth-to-the-max,
Who are stopped and forgotten on untrodden tracks

We lack what we're not and we have what we are
Though tidings of progress are less near than far
For we know all things good must come to an end
But of the converse we all do pretend

One needs a friend, with whom one can share
The ins and the outs, the why and the where
But the universe ignores our wherefore and tear,
For when we are watching, there's nobody there.

What is a rough path without the headlights ahead and the occasional glances behind?
